Home > Exhibitions Life in Universe

"Life in the Universe" will send you on a journey of discovery to the farthest corners of the cosmos. To stars from the distant past, billions of years old, and to planets that may or may not support life. Along the way, you'll also learn where you came from. After all, you are made of the same tiny particles as those distant stars and planets. Through interactive exhibits, hands-on games, and stunning visuals, you'll learn about planets, stars, and the search for alien life, inspiring young explorers to imagine the future of discovery.
